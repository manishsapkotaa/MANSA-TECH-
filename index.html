Who We Are

Mansa Technology Pvt. Ltd. is a leading IT solutions provider based at Jorpati Sundarijal Bus Stop, Kathmandu. We specialize in CCTV installations, electrical services, computer and printer repairs, audio system setups, and comprehensive networking solutions.

With years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality services and products to both residential and commercial clients. Our team of skilled technicians ensures professional installation and reliable after-sales support.

We stock a wide range of premium products including Cat6 cables, RJ45/RJ11 connectors, multi-plugs, branded NVRs from Hikvision, Tiandy and Dahua, computer peripherals, PoE switches, and professional CCTV cameras. All our products come with warranty and authentic certifications.
